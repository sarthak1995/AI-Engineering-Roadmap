# Payment And Refund Policy

Payment issues include duplicate charges, failed checkout, missing refunds,
incorrect billing, blocked funds, and card authorization holds.

Cases involving blocked money, duplicate charges, missing refunds, or inability
to complete checkout should be high urgency. Fraud, stolen cards, and
unauthorized transactions should be critical.

Support should collect the order number, payment method type, approximate charge
amount, charge date, and whether the customer sees a pending authorization or a
posted charge.

Recommended response:

- Acknowledge the billing concern.
- Explain whether the case appears to involve a charge, hold, refund, or failed
  payment.
- Escalate fraud or unauthorized transactions immediately.
- Do not ask for full card numbers.
