# Delivery Support Policy

Delivery issues include late orders, missing orders, wrong delivery addresses,
damaged packages, incomplete grocery delivery, and orders marked delivered that
the customer did not receive.

Missing deliveries for groceries, medicine, baby formula, or other urgent items
should be high urgency, especially when the customer needs the order today or
has already been charged.

Support should collect the order number, delivery window, address confirmation,
driver notes if available, and whether the customer checked nearby safe places.

Recommended response:

- Apologize for the delivery problem.
- Confirm that support will investigate the delivery scan and charge.
- Offer replacement, refund review, or escalation depending on policy.
- Avoid promising a refund until the case is reviewed.
