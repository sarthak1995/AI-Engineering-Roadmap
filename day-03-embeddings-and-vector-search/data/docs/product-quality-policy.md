# Product Quality Policy

Product quality issues include damaged products, expired food, wrong items,
missing items, defective products, poor substitutions, and quality complaints.

Most product quality issues are medium urgency unless they involve safety,
allergens, injury, medicine, baby products, or perishable goods needed urgently.
Safety or injury concerns should be critical.

Support should collect the product name, order number, photos if available,
expiration date if relevant, and whether the customer wants a refund,
replacement, or store follow-up.

Recommended response:

- Apologize for the product issue.
- Ask for product and order details if missing.
- Offer the next review step for refund or replacement.
- Escalate safety, injury, or allergen reports.
