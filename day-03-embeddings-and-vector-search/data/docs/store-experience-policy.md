# Store Experience Policy

Store experience issues include rude associate behavior, long checkout lines,
incorrect shelf prices, pickup delays, store cleanliness, accessibility
problems, and general complaints about an in-store visit.

Most store experience issues are medium urgency. Issues involving safety,
harassment, discrimination, threats, injury, or legal concerns should be
critical.

Support should collect the store location, visit date and time, department,
receipt or order number if available, and a concise description of what
happened.

Recommended response:

- Thank the customer for reporting the experience.
- Ask for store and timing details if missing.
- Route serious safety or discrimination concerns to escalation.
- Avoid making employment or disciplinary promises.
