# Account Security Policy

Customers should contact support immediately if they suspect account takeover,
unauthorized access, password changes they did not request, or suspicious order
activity.

Security-related issues should be treated as critical when there is evidence of
fraud, identity theft, account takeover, stolen payment credentials, or blocked
access to an account with active orders.

Support should ask for only the minimum details needed to investigate. Never ask
customers to share passwords, full card numbers, one-time passcodes, or private
authentication tokens.

Recommended response:

- Acknowledge the risk.
- Confirm that the case is being escalated.
- Ask the customer to change their password from the official account page.
- Advise them to review recent orders and payment methods.
- Route the case to the account security queue.
